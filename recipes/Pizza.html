Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza recipe is easy to make and customizable to your taste. With a crispy crust, flavorful sauce, and your favorite toppings, it's sure to be a hit with family and friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.)
  • Cornmeal or flour, for dusting

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface into a round shape of desired thickness.
  3. Sprinkle cornmeal or flour on a pizza peel or the back of a baking sheet to prevent sticking. Transfer the rolled-out dough to the peel or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, covering the entire pizza.
  6. Add your desired toppings evenly over the cheese.
  7. Transfer the pizza to the preheated pizza stone in the oven, or place the baking sheet directly on the oven rack.
  8. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
